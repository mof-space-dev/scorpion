# SCORPION - Generative Art

An authentic, primal digital artwork created with p5.js, evoking an ancient scorpion cave painting.

This piece features a bold, raw sketch style in ochre and pitch black, capturing a martial scorpion on a textured "wall." It pulses with a raw, unsettling energy.

The work is defined by its dramatic **negative/positive color inversion**, subtle analog scanline/film grain effects, and custom glitch transitions, creating the feeling of viewing a forgotten, unstable ancient transmission.

## Colors
- Primitive Ochre
- Pitch Black
- Natural Bone White (Glitch/Flash Color)

## Technologies
- [p5.js](https://p5js.org/)
